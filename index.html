Glassfiber Reinforced Polyester

It is a thermosetting composite of polyester resin & glassfiber to form a structural matrix. G.R.P. has excellent strength-weight ratio, and can be molded into the most complex shapes and durable formation.

Glassfiber Reinforced Cement

It is a composite Mixture of Cement, Fine Aggregate and AR-Glassfiber. G.R.C. is widely used in the construction owing to its flexible ability, versatility in apperance, texture, as well as acoustic and fire performance..

Glassfiber Reinforced Gypsum

It is a Composite Mixture of Alpha Gypsum,  AR-Glassfiber and Additives. GRG is a Lighweight, yet Strong Architectural Material with Excellent Flame Resistant. Its Installation is Quic and Relatively Easy.
